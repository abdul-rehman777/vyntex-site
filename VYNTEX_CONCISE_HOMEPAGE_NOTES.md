# VYNTEX Concise Homepage Refinement

## Objective
Make the homepage faster to understand, easier to scan, more relevant to VYNTEX's actual offer, and more conversion-focused without changing the brand or backend systems.

## Final homepage flow
1. Concise hero with one clear positioning statement and two CTAs
2. Connected business dashboard preview
3. Four core systems VYNTEX connects
4. Disconnected-tools comparison paired with a real customer workflow
5. Four featured industry examples
6. Four reasons to choose VYNTEX plus one concise qualification statement
7. Three focused pricing starting points
8. Four-step implementation process
9. Final consultation CTA

## What was removed or merged
- Removed the separate long qualification section
- Merged the comparison and workflow sections into one stronger story
- Reduced industries from six cards to four featured cards
- Reduced pricing preview from four cards to three
- Reduced workflow from eight steps to six
- Reduced comparison lists from seven items per side to four
- Removed repeated benefit language and excess section spacing

## Positioning used
VYNTEX is presented as a connected business systems and technology implementation partner that connects CRM, communication, scheduling, payments, automation, websites, and reporting around the client's actual workflow.

## Primary conversion path
Primary CTA: Book a Consultation
Secondary CTA: See How It Works
