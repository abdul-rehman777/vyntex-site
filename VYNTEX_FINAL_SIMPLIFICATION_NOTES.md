# VYNTEX Final Simplification

## Core direction
The homepage was rebuilt around one immediate message: Vyntex connects the systems a service business uses to manage customers, communication, scheduling, payments, automation, websites, marketing, and reporting.

## Homepage flow
1. Hero with one headline, one explanation, two actions, and a compact dashboard visual
2. Three clear service groups
3. Before-and-after comparison
4. Three-step implementation process
5. Compact industry list
6. Final consultation call to action

## Removed from the homepage
- Pricing cards
- Long feature grids
- Repeated workflow explanations
- Separate qualification section
- Detailed industry bullet lists
- Excess trust and benefit cards
- Repeated consultation explanations

## Navigation
The main navigation now contains only:
- Services
- Industries
- Partners
- About
- Contact
- Client Login / Client Portal
- Book Consultation

Solutions, Resources, and Pricing were removed from navigation. `/pricing` now redirects to `/services`, where prices already live. `/how-it-works` redirects to the homepage process section.

## About page
The founder block and all references to Daysi were removed. The page now focuses only on Vyntex, its operating principles, and its approach.

## Footer
The newsletter and redundant resource columns were removed. The footer now contains a concise consultation CTA, key services, company links, verified contact details, language control, and legal links.
